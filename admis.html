Admissions

Learn about entrance requirements, enrollment steps, deadlines, and qualifications for joining the College of Engineering.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Form 138 or Report Card
  • Good Moral Certificate
  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • Two 2x2 ID Photos
  • Entrance Examination

Enrollment Steps

  1. Fill out the admission form online or onsite.
  2. Submit all documentary requirements.
  3. Take the entrance exam.
  4. Attend the interview (if required).
  5. Wait for approval and enrollment schedule.
